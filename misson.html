WHO ARE WE?
Jain Community Services (JCS) is a dedicated organization committed to providing comprehensive support and assistance to the Jain community in Canada. Our multifaceted approach encompasses a range of services aimed at enhancing the lives of Jain individuals. From facilitating job placements to assisting with housing solutions, JCS strives to empower members of the Jain community by addressing their diverse needs. Our efforts extend beyond the professional realm, encompassing social integration, cultural preservation, and overall community well-being. By fostering a sense of belonging and offering practical assistance, JCS is dedicated to creating a supportive environment where Jain individuals can thrive and build a fulfilling life in Canada.
